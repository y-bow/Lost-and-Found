## ğŸš€ Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- University email address
- Valid student/staff ID

### User Flow

1. **Registration/Login**
   - Enter your full name
   - Provide your university email
   - Enter your ID number
   - Add your contact number
   - Click "Enter Portal"

2. **Browse Items**
   - Use the search bar to find specific items
   - Click on category icons to filter items
   - View "All Items" for complete listings

3. **Post an Item**
   - Click the "Post Item" button
   - Select item status (Lost or Found)
   - Choose the appropriate category
   - Fill in item details (title, description, location, date)
   - Upload an image (optional)
   - Submit your posting

4. **Manage Your Items**
   - View your posted items on the dashboard
   - Track the status of your reports
   - Update or remove items as needed
